The Death Of



Tupac Shakur


Born in East Harlem, New York City, NY

June 16, 1971
Assassinated in Las Vegas, NV (Died in hospital)
September 13, 1996





                Tupac Shakur was a successful hip-hop artist hailing from New York City, although most commonly affiliated with the West Coast. On September 7th, 1996 at 11:15PM the car that Tupac was in stopped at a red light (East Flamingo Road and Koval Lane). A white Cadillac pulled up on their right side and rapidly fired gunshots through their open window at Shakur. Tupac was hit 4 times. He was hit twice in the chest, once in the thigh, and once in the arm. One of the shots to his chest hit him in the right lung.
